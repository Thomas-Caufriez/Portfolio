Dyna-livres

Dyna-livres was my first important school project (semptember 2023 - june 2024).

I made it in collaboration with Wassim Jniouen, Marie-Ange Bouchat and Meryem Turkoz.

We had to re-imagine a whole website of an association and to code at least 3 pages in HTML and CSS.

First, we met through a phone call the manager of the association. She told us what she didn't want on the website and what was vital.

Based on those requests, we've built a model on Figma and then coded it.
